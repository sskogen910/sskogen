Projects

I moved into this house in 2016, so I’ve been here about 10 years now, and my garden space has changed a lot during that time. In the beginning, there was no fence, but as my ambition grew, the fence did too—slowly extending further down the side of the house to include more space. I’ve used the garden in different ways and experimented with various methods I’ve learned along the way—not always with great success, but every failed attempt has taught me something.
