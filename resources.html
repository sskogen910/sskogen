Helpful Gardening Resources

Below are books, tools, and websites that have helped me learn and grow as a gardener.

Resource Name Type Description
Carrots Love Tomatoes Book Focuses on companion planting and how different plants can support each other’s growth.
Permaculture in Pots Book Shows how to grow food in small spaces using permaculture principles and container gardening.
Small-Scale Homesteading Book A guide to sustainable living, including gardening, food preservation, and small homestead practices.
Gardening with Chickens Book Explains how to integrate chickens into a garden system for natural fertilization and pest control.
Vertical Gardening Book Teaches how to maximize growing space by gardening upward instead of outward.
Stirrup Loop Hoe Tool A versatile garden hoe used for weeding and cultivating soil with less effort.
GreenStalk Vertical Planter Tool A vertical planter system designed for growing multiple plants in a small footprint.
Garden Kneeler Tool Provides comfort and support while kneeling or sitting during gardening tasks.
SeedTime Website Offers planning tools and resources to help organize planting schedules and garden layouts.
The Hollar Homestead Blog Shares practical homesteading and gardening tips from real-life experience.
The Grassfed Homestead Blog Focuses on sustainable living, gardening, and raising animals on a small scale.
Sow the Land Blog Documents homesteading and gardening projects with a focus on learning and experimentation.
Justin Rhodes Website Provides resources and courses focused on permaculture and sustainable living.
Misfit Gardening Blog Offers unconventional gardening advice and encourages learning through mistakes.
You Grow Girl Blog A creative and modern take on gardening with helpful guides and inspiration.
